Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Datomancer (datomancer.com) collects, uses, and protects data when you visit our site.

1. Information We Collect

We don’t collect personal data like names, emails, or IP addresses. The only data we process comes from our analytics tool:

  • Page views
  • Referrer URLs
  • Device type and browser
  • Geographic region (at the country level only)

2. Analytics via Plausible

We use Plausible Analytics, a GDPR- and CCPA-compliant service that does not use cookies or collect personal identifiers. All metrics are aggregated and anonymous.

3. Cookies

Datomancer itself does not set any cookies. Plausible may set a minimal, first-party cookie only to distinguish unique visitors over time, and it contains no personal data.

4. Third-Party Links

Our posts may include links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately before interacting.

5. Data Retention

Plausible retains aggregated analytics for up to 365 days. We do not store or share raw event-level data yourself on our servers.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect our site and any data we process through Plausible. However, no system is completely secure—use at your own risk.

7. Children’s Privacy

Datomancer is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe we have, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Email us at talk@datomancer.com.
